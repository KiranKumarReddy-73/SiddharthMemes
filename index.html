History Of Our Page

It all started in May 2019, Dhasarath created this page to share college updates in a fun way. Over time, Likith and Pavan Teja took the initiative to develop and grow it further.

Siddharth Memes is the FIRST and OG meme page of our college! Seeing its success, many other pages were inspired to follow. From small beginnings to hitting major milestones, this page has grown into a community of 12K+ followers, keeping students entertained, informed, and engaged!
